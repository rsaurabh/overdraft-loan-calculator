True overdraft model: Your full loan limit sits in the account from approval date — interest is ₹0 until you withdraw. Each withdrawal draws down the limit and starts interest. Deposits reduce the drawn balance and save interest. Monthly interest is auto-debited on the 1st of every month. Add EMI payments manually as Deposit transactions.
